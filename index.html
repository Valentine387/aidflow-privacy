Privacy Policy

AidFlow ("we", "us", or "our") operates a WhatsApp-based field data collection platform for non-governmental organizations (NGOs). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our service.

By using AidFlow via WhatsApp, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information you provide directly

  • Your full name and job title
  • Your WhatsApp phone number
  • Your email address
  • Your geographic location (district, sub-district, region)
  • Field data you submit through our forms (CEP session data, attendance records, observations)

1.2 Information collected automatically

  • Message timestamps and delivery status
  • Form submission timestamps
  • Language preference

1.3 Information we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect personal data of beneficiaries or community members
  • We do not access your WhatsApp contacts or chat history
  • We do not collect financial information
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery: To operate the WhatsApp bot and process your form submissions
  • Data collection: To record and store field data on behalf of your NGO
  • Reporting: To generate Word/Excel reports and send them to your registered email
  • Communication: To send submission confirmations and correction notifications via WhatsApp
  • Quality control: To enable supervisors to review and approve data corrections
  • Analytics: To provide NGO dashboards and Tableau-compatible data exports
We never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Where data is stored

All data is stored in secure PostgreSQL databases. Depending on your organization's configuration, data may be stored on local servers or cloud infrastructure (Google Cloud Platform).

3.2 Security measures

  • All data transmissions use HTTPS encryption
  • Database access is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Passwords are stored using secure hashing
  • Audit trails are maintained for all data modifications
  • Access tokens expire and are regularly rotated

3.3 Data retention

Field data is retained for the duration of the NGO project plus a minimum of 5 years for donor compliance purposes. Personal profile data is retained while your account is active.

4. WhatsApp and Meta

AidFlow uses the WhatsApp Business API, operated by Meta Platforms, Inc. When you interact with our bot:

  • Messages are transmitted through Meta's WhatsApp infrastructure
  • Meta's own Privacy Policy applies to the WhatsApp service itself
  • We only receive and store the content of messages you send to our bot
  • We do not have access to your personal WhatsApp account or other conversations

Meta's Privacy Policy can be found at: whatsapp.com/legal/privacy-policy

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: You can update your profile data by typing PROFILE on WhatsApp
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: You can request your data in Excel or CSV format
  • Opt-out: You can stop using the service by typing STOP on WhatsApp

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the details below.

6. Data Sharing

Your data may be shared in the following limited circumstances:

  • Within your NGO: Supervisors and project managers can view submissions made by their field workers
  • Donors/Funders: Aggregated, anonymized reports may be shared with project donors as required by grant agreements
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data if required by law or court order
Individual field worker data is never shared outside your organization without your explicit consent.

7. Children's Privacy

AidFlow is designed for use by adult field workers and NGO staff. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify active users of significant changes via WhatsApp message. Continued use of the service after notification constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
